About PPF

Tax Benefits

PPF offers triple tax benefits - investment is tax-deductible, interest earned is tax-free, and maturity amount is tax-free.

Lock-in Period

PPF has a lock-in period of 15 years. Partial withdrawals are allowed after 7 years for specific purposes.

Interest Rate

PPF interest rate is set by the government quarterly. Current rate is 7.1% per annum, compounded annually.
